## Vision

BitFlow aims to become a leading decentralized lending protocol on the Stacks blockchain, providing:

- **Secure** and **transparent** borrowing/lending
- **Bitcoin-backed** stability through Stacks' Proof-of-Transfer
- **Permissionless** access to DeFi services
- **Community-driven** governance and development

**Timeline:** Q1-Q2 2026  
**Status:** Planning

### 2.1: Multi-Asset Support

Support for multiple cryptocurrencies as collateral and loan assets.

**Planned Assets:**
- sBTC (Bitcoin on Stacks)
- USDA (Stablecoin)
- Other SIP-010 tokens

**Features:**
- Multi-asset collateral ratios
- Cross-asset liquidation logic
- Enhanced oracle integration

### 2.2: Dynamic Interest Rates

Implement utilization-based interest rate models where rates adjust based on supply and demand.

### 2.3: Loan Extensions

Allow borrowers to extend loan terms with extension fees.

### 2.4: Partial Repayments

Enable repaying loans in installments with proportional collateral release.
